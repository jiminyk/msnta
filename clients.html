Clients

Through our many years of experience in this unique industry
of non-emergency medical transportation, we have learned what works effectively and what does not. Every aspect of our program has been evaluated and refined, and we constantly look for ways to enhance our services to benefit our clients and overall member experience. Most recently we developed a proprietary software application, Trip2, specifically built to manage non-emergency medical transportation. Trip2 was built from the depth of experience in the industry to
better manage NEMT services. The mobile application allows for riders to schedule reservations and track their driver. Additionally, the rider can see who their driver is and even rate their driver to ensure satisfaction.

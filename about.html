About us

Mobility Solutions Network is a Transportation management company based in Miami-Dade County, that has been providing high quality non-emergency medical transportation services since 1995. MSN and our family of companies coordinate over 3 million trips a year, and our commitment to quality, safety and cost-efficiency has earned us the trust of many clients in both the public and private sector. We have extensive experience in servicing the medically fragile, disabled and elderly population enrolled in Medicaid and Medicare advantage organizations and health care facilities. MSN provides call intake, routing, driver/vehicle credentialing, vehicle maintenance and repair, complaint management, reporting and other services that support our clients and their members.

Our Technology

Through our many years of experience in this unique industry of non-emergency transportation, we have learned what works effectively and what does not. Every aspect of our program has been evaluated and refined, and we constantly look for ways to enhance our services to benefit our clients and overall member experience. 

Most recently we developed a proprietary software application, Trip2, specifically built to manage non-emergency medical transportation. Trip2 was built from the depth of experience in the industry to better manage NET services. The mobile application allows for riders to schedule reservations and track their driver. Additionally, the rider can see who their driver is and even rate their driver to ensure satisfaction. 

Trip2 features:

  • Improved passenger interfaces including booking and tracking
  • Effective utilization of transportation network
  • Vehicle Tracking and digital signature to reduce fraud
  • Centralized Reporting and Tracking
  • Meets HIPAA and privacy requirements
  • Covered service verification
  • Claims Processing and adjudication
  • Complaints collection, investigation, tracking and reporting
  • Route Optimization

Our Network

We understand the value of a high-quality transportation network and the importance of communication at all levels.  MSN provides ongoing support to ensure our network is capable of providing quality transportation services to our clients. One important aspect of managing our network is sharing and reviewing performance data in efforts to drive improvements. From experience, we realize the importance of ongoing analysis of the program given the constant changes in travel patterns, membership shifts, utilization and modes of transport. All of these changes impact the network and more specifically how we use the resources to best serve our clients.

We have the ability to expand our service area statewide given our scalable network model composed of owned vehicles, private providers, public transit and our independent drivers (Trip2).  Our Trip2 network of independent drivers is a key component of our network. Trip2 drivers meet all of the AHCA NEMT program standards and follow the same strict credentialing as a private provider, which includes:

  • Passenger Service and Safety
  • Disability Awareness
  • ‍Defensive Driving
  • ‍HIPAA privacy requirements
  • ‍First Aid/CPR
  • Drug screening
  • ‍Background checks
  • ‍ADA requirements
  • ‍FWA reporting
